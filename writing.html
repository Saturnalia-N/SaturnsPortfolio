The Sky's The Limit

For the past 3 years I have been writing a book about a crew of young adults that go looking for the most sought after thing in the world.
It is a castle that holds the most valuable tresures in the world but nobody knows where it is most people think its fake but the main character's crew belives that it is out there somewhere.

Heres The Introduction Pharagraph:

Have you ever heard about the castle? Well, the story goes like this:
there is a castle that is said to contain riches and items beyond human understanding,
things that could destroy the world or bring peace depending on how you want to use it.
Things that could grant wishes or power, the castle was said to be filled with these items that
together could buy the world, though this is all a story that some believe or a dream that was said to not exist,
there was one man who found it but vanished before revealing its true location.
With this information, thousands of people have set out to find this castle but will anyone really find it
or will it remain a mystery forever?

Story Details

These details are more for my own pleasure to write down as I enjoy sharing small details about what I'm creating with others!

In this story the world building is more like dnd but also like normal life. You could refrence the world of dragon ball a little, although it feels like the world is very old it is also highly advanced with very good tech but it also has its power aspect
although this story is set in the past with magic like dnd there is not as many races as dnd. Though the world also has very advanced tech it is not in all parts of the world the tools come up often but things like cars or telephones dont show up everywhere.
I think this world really works in the sense of magic and they would find a way to integrate it into their everyday tools which make them a lot more advanced while still being in the past with a lot more discoverable things in the future.

Character details

The Two Main Characters:

we start the story seeing the two main characters as little kids talking under the stary night. That night they promise eachother that they would do the impossible and find the castle from the fairy tail. After a time skip we see them heading out on their quest.
